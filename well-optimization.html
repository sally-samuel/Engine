Well Optimization & Downhole Services

To provide a one-stop solutions platform for turnkey drilling and well optimization for our clients, we adopted the vaunted 'extended enterprise concept' and went into a strategic partnership with Wellsmart Drilling to form LeveneSmart Limited, an entity that provides end-to-end drilling and well optimization services.

Drilling and Evaluation

Our partners' fleet of rigs range from 750 HP to 3,000 HP and offer drilling capabilities for all type of wells with depths up to 30,000 ft.

Our comprehensive line of oilfield solutions addresses fishing requirements for wells, from milling, casing patches, workover projects, and abandonment and casing exit to open hole fishing.

We offer integrated well testing services in the exploration, appraisal and development phases of oil and gas wells. Our aim is to provide newer, faster and more precise testing results though innovation and superior service quality.

Our partners' large fleet of logging trucks, offshore units, logging tools and pressure control equipment, provides a wide variety of open-hole and cased-hole logging services to our clients.

We provide drilling fluid systems and related technologies to the oil and gas industry in accordance with international standards and regulations for both onshore and offshore projects.

Our slickline services cover: basic removal of scale, wax and sand build-up, setting plugs, changing out gas lift valves, fishing and other complex well applications. From basic to most complex slickline applications.

Our directional drilling services (LWD, RSS, MOTORS & MWD) provide a whole suite of solutions from conventional to unconventional drilling applications. From simple deviated to long horizontal wells, our directional drilling services meet our customer's targets cost effectively.

Production Services

We recognize the importance of well integrity for our customers, especially as wells are becoming more and more complex. Through a comprehensive approach to engineering, planning, execution and post job evaluation, we ensure the highest degree of success.

We have extensive coiled tubing experience such as stimulation, nitrogen lifting, fishing, milling, clean-out, scale removal, through-tubing plug setting, horizontal logging and other complex well applications.

Each of our locations worldwide possess a central laboratory. These base laboratories are operated by qualified personnel who provide support and services to all the mobile labs in all the sites where we work.

Our wide range of chemical solutions enable the treatment of both sandstone and dolomite formations under the toughest reservoir conditions.

Matrix stimulation, hydraulic fracturing and pumping services are provided throughout all our operations utilizing modern technology and state-of-the-art equipment.

A full portfolio of drilling fluid systems and related technologies for the oil and gas industry.

Surface and downhole safety, high-pressure, and a wide range of completions solutions.

We provide filtration services to our clients to meet their requirements, save additional costs of well stimulation and avoid related operational problems due to non-filtered fluids, especially across the pay zone.
