Corporate Social Responsibility

Our Commitment

At Shefa Engineering, we recognise that sustainable business success goes hand-in-hand with responsible corporate citizenship. Our CSR approach is embedded in everything we do.

Our People

We work to reduce the carbon footprint by reducing flared gas, and investing in renewable energy research and technology that limits/eliminates negative environmental impacts.

Host Communities

We support host communities work by identifying and implementing projects that benefit the communities. We employ, equip, and train from the communities.
We are successful when host communities are positively impacted.

Government

We support Government’s Local Content, Indigenous Capacity Building Policies.

Environment

We work to reduce our carbon footprint by minimising flared gas and investing in renewable energy research and technology. Our environmental initiatives aim to limit or eliminate negative impacts from our operations, contributing to a cleaner future.

Committed to responsible operations in every community we serve.
