Who We Are

Shefa Engineering

Shefa Engineering Limited (Shefa) is a wholly indigenous company that provides Oilfield Service to the oil & gas industry in Nigeria and globally. We offer a range of services, from Engineering, Procurement and Construction (EPC) to Drilling & Production, Marine, Facilities Operations and Maintenance Services.

At Shefa Engineering, we are committed to closing the energy demand gap. Our goal is to be at the forefront of gas energy distribution, ensuring end-users attain clean energy at a relatively low cost. Our franchise area initiation is set to meet the gas demand of companies, residences and complexes within Nigeria and beyond. Our services cover engineering construction and pipeline solutions.
